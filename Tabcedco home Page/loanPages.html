Loan Schemes

GENERAL TERM LOAN SCHEME
To start small business / trade loan is sanctioned to the individuals.

Maximum loan limit is Rs. 15.00 lakhs at the rate of 6-8% p.a.

New Swarnima Scheme
To start small business / trade loan is sanctioned to the individuals

Maximum Loan limit is Rs.2.00 lakhs at the rate of 5% p.a.

Mahila Samridi Yojana
Loan assistance is sanctioned to members of Women Self Help Groups

Maximum Loan limit is Rs. 1,00,000/- per beneficiary and Rs. 15.00 lakhs per Self Help Group at the rate of 4% p.a.

MICRO CREDIT SCHEME FOR MEN
Loan assistance is sanctioned to members of men Self Help Groups.

Maximum Loan limit is Rs. 1,00,000/- per beneficiary and Rs. 15.00 lakhs per Self Help Group at the rate of 5% p.a.

MILCH ANIMAL SCHEME
Loan will be sanctioned to a beneficiary for purchase of two milch animals.

Loan upto Rs.20.00 lacs for Domestic Courses & Rs.30.00 lacs for courses Abroad at 3-8% p.a.
