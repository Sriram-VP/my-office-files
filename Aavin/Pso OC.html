  1. In the Government order first read above, Government have issued orders approving the implementation of ‘Milch Animal Scheme’ to the benefit of BCs/MBCs/DNCs members in Milk Producers Co-operative Societies through the TABCEDCO in consultation with Tamil Nadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation Limited inorder to uplift the economic status of them and with a view to enhance rural employment opportunities and to increase the milk production capacity in the state and to sanction the term loan assistance subject to the conditions laid down between the TABCEDCO and NBCFDC from time to time. It is also ordered that the Managing Director, Tamil Nadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation Limited, Chennai-51 shall arrange for the selection of Beneficiary, Milch Animal, Marketability of the produce and the recovery of loan amount with interest etc. from the beneficiaries and remit the same to TABCEDCO.

  2. In Para 5 of the said G.O. the Government have ordered further that TABCEDCO. shall release Term Loan Assistance to the beneficiaries identified under the scheme (‘Milch Animal Induction Programme’) through the Tamilnadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation Limited out of the term loan assistance provided by NBCFDC., NewDelhi from time to time as per the conditions laid down between these two Corporations.

The loan is sanctioned subject to the following terms and conditions:-


  1. The loan amount should be utilized for the purpose for which it has been sanctioned.

  2. The beneficiaries should be the member of Primary Milk Co-operative Societies / the District Co-operative Milk Union and they belong to BCs/MBCs/DNCs as approved by Tamil Nadu Government. Their annual income of the beneficiaries should not exceed Rs.3,00,000/- both in Rural areas and Rs.3,00,000/- in Urban areas.

  3. The total project cost for one milch animal is Rs.30000/- inclusive of insurance coverage and other incidental charges for the purchase of one animal.
4.
  1. A cow qualifies for selection when its average yield is around 8 litres of milk per day.

  2. Each cow should have a calf preferably a heifer.

  3. Pregnant cows and cows beyond third lactation should not bepurchased.

  4. Purchase of hi-breed cows like Jercy etc., from other States should be encouraged.

  5. Any other terms and conditions prescribed by the Federation / District Union shall also be followed.

  6. A Committee comprising of a Veternarian of the Milk Union, the beneficiary and the District Backward Classes and Minorities Welfare Officer should assist for selecting and translocate the cows upon purchase.
  1. The term loan assistance to the beneficiaries under this scheme will be released to the Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited through the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Milk Producers Federation Limited, Chennai-51 (95% loan + 5% Beneficiary Share), in two equal installments in continuance with the Condition No.3 mentioned supra.

  2. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should arrange for scrutinizing and identifying the eligible beneficiaries, milch animals, marketability of produce and recovery of loan with interest from the beneficiaries and remit the same to TABCEDCO.

  3. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should take necessary steps and safeguarding measures for proper implementation and monitoring of the project.

  4. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should obtain income and community certificate of the beneficiaries issued by the competent authority and should be kept for verification at any time.

  5. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should make all endeavours to take comprehensive/insurance policy coverage for the assets,i.e. milch animals, covered by the project, in the joint name of District Milk Union and the owners.

  6. The period of repayment of loan is over a period of 3 years. The union may collect the installment amount (Principal and Interest) from the beneficiaries either monthly or quarterly basis. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should arrange to recover the loan amount with interest thereon. Incase of default of repayment by the beneficiaries, the Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should initiate suitable legal proceedings to recover the loan.

  7. The Salem Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should maintain documents in proof of the social backwardness of the beneficiaries and also recovery details of the loans disbursed by them. Hence, the documents of proof and details of loan amount disbursed should be kept in the custody of respective Co-operative Milk Union.

  8. The interest will be calculated from the date of disbursement of loan amount by TABCEDCO. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should disburse the loan amount to the beneficiaries within the reasonable time, failing which the milk union should pay penal interest of 13.5% per annum to TABCEDCO along with the undisbursed portion of such amount remaining with milk unions. The union should also inform the utilization of loan in form prescribed to TABCEDCO (copy enclosed).

  9. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should start the repayment of loan to TABCEDCO after the expiry of two months moratorium period.

  10. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should obtain sufficient surety/security as per the by laws of the union from the beneficiaries before disbursement of loan.

  11. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, should remit the amounts recovered from the beneficiaries to TABCEDCO once in a quarter periodically till completion of the loan together with interest. The repayment period of loan by the union to TABCEDCO will be 12 quarterly installments. (Total repayment period is 3 years).

  12. The first installment of repayment of loan shall commence from the Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, after the completion of the holiday period of 2 months from the date of issue of cheque. In case of default in repayment of principal and interest on due dates, additional interest at 5% besides the normal subsidised interest of 6% will be charged on the installments not paid on due dates.

  13. Irrespective of whether the installment due/dues are collected from the beneficiaries, the installment/dues should be remitted to TABCEDCO by Milk Union on the due date. All the legal/miscellaneous expenses incurred towards the recovery/realization of the dues shall be borne by the District Milk Union. However, both the society and the district union are at liberty to levy and collect the penal interest as per their norms against default payment by the beneficiaries.

  14. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, is entitled to get 3% interest collected( Out of 6%) for the service rendered by the Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, such as scrutiny, processing of applications, sanctioning and disbursing the loan, recoveries made from the beneficiaries and legal expenses if any incurred, postage and stationery and recovery/collection of remittance of installment dues on the due date, irrespective of actual physical recovery being effected or not by which the risk if any involved in such recovery shall be sole liability of union. The TABCEDCO shall part with this 3% (Three Parts) out of the interest collected @ 6% by the Union.

  15. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, shall abide the various instructions issued by TABCEDCO from time to time, during the implementation of the scheme.

  16. In the event of any dispute arises in respect of any one of terms and conditions mentioned above it is mutually agreed that the same shall be referred to an arbitrator for arbitration and the award passed shall be binding on all the parties. However in all matters the Court at Chennai shall have jurisdiction.

  17. The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union Limited, i.e. authorised person of said institution shall sign this Memorandum of Agreement-Cum-Provisional Sanction Order in each page (by affixing the seal of the institutions) as acceptance for the receipt of the loan amount in accordance with the said terms and conditions, and return the same to TABCEDCO.
