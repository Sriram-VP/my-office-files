ORDER:

In continuation of this office Memorandum of Agreement-cum-Provisional Sanction Order vide reference 3th cited, a sum of Rs.1,35,09,000/- has been sanctioned to Salem DCMPU for purchase of 474 Milch Animal to 237 beneficiaries. In the reference 6th cited, Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers Union has requested to release 2ed spell amount of Rs.67,54,500/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakh Fifty Four Thousands And Five Hundred Only) towards the term loan to 237 beneficiaries for the purchase of 237 milch animals. Accordingly sanction is hereby accorded for the payment of Term Loan of Rs.67,54,500/- (Rupees Sixty Seven Lakh Fifty Four Thousands And Five Hundred Only) to Salem DCMPU for purchase of animal for 237 beneficiaries in accordance with the terms and conditions incorporated in the Memorandum of Agreement-Cum- Provisional Sanction Order already accepted and also subject to the following conditions:-

i ) That the beneficiaries are belonging to Backward Classes/Most Backward Classes and their Annual Income is below the prescribed limit and

ii ) That the Utilisation certificate and asset creation certificate have to be furnished to this Corporation, within a month on receipt of funds from this Corporation.

Attention is also invited to the following conditions stipulated in the Memorandum of Agreement-cum-Provisional Sanction Order for strict adherence.

Condition No.12:

The said Union should disburse the loan amount to the beneficiaries immediately. At any cost it should disburse the loan within a period of 30 days failing which on the expiry period of 30 days, the Union shall pay penal interest of 13.5% per annum to TABCEDCO along with the undisbursed portion of such amount remaining with the Union. The Union, after disbursement of the loan, should ensure, assets creation, immediately following asset creation, TABCEDCO shall be informed in the relevant Form (Form–B).


Condition No.14:

The Salem District Co-operative Milk Producers’ Union Limited, should obtain sufficient surety/security as per the existing procedure being followed by the Union from the beneficiaries before disbursement of loan.


Condition No.16:

1. That the Union shall pay the Penal interest at the rate of 5% in addition to normal rate of interest for the defaulted period to TABCEDCO.
